# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for Pay-Tracker

## Problem Statement
Developers and businesses often struggle with managing billing and invoicing processes. Tracking payment statuses, filtering invoices, and managing collections can be time-consuming and error-prone. There is a need for a streamlined system that simplifies these processes, ensuring timely payments and reducing administrative overhead.

## Target Users
- **Freelancers and Independent Developers**: Individuals who need to manage their invoices and payments efficiently.
- **Small to Medium Businesses (SMBs)**: Companies that require a robust system to handle multiple invoices and track payment statuses.
- **Accounting Teams**: Teams responsible for managing financial transactions and ensuring timely payments.

## Goals
- **Simplify Payment Tracking**: Provide a user-friendly interface for tracking payment statuses.
- **Enhance Filtering Capabilities**: Allow users to filter invoices based on various criteria such as date, amount, and client.
- **Improve Collections**: Facilitate the collections process by providing reminders and follow-up tools.
- **Reduce Administrative Overhead**: Automate repetitive tasks to save time and reduce errors.

## Key Features (Prioritized)
1. **Invoice Management**
   - Create and manage invoices with customizable templates.
   - Track payment statuses (paid, pending, overdue).
   - Generate and send invoices to clients.

2. **Payment Tracking**
   - Real-time updates on payment statuses.
   - Notifications for upcoming and overdue payments.
   - Detailed payment history for each invoice.

3. **Filtering and Search**
   - Filter invoices by date range, amount, client, and payment status.
   - Search functionality to quickly find specific invoices.

4. **Collections Tools**
   - Automated reminders for upcoming and overdue payments.
   - Follow-up tools to facilitate collections.
   - Customizable reminder templates.

5. **Reporting and Analytics**
   - Generate reports on payment trends and collections efficiency.
   - Analytics dashboard to track key metrics such as average payment time and overdue invoices.

6. **Integration Capabilities**
   - Integrate with popular accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks, Xero).
   - API access for custom integrations.

## Success Metrics
- **User Adoption**: Number of active users and frequency of use.
- **Efficiency**: Reduction in time spent on invoice management and collections.
- **Accuracy**: Decrease in errors related to payment tracking and invoicing.
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Feedback from users on the ease of use and effectiveness of the system.
- **Revenue Impact**: Increase in timely payments and reduction in overdue invoices.

## Scope
- **In-Scope**
  - Development of a web-based application for invoice management and payment tracking.
  - Implementation of filtering and search capabilities.
  - Creation of automated reminders and follow-up tools.
  - Integration with popular accounting software.
  - Development of reporting and analytics features.

- **Out-of-Scope**
  - Mobile application development.
  - Advanced financial forecasting tools.
  - Multi-currency support (Phase 2).
  - Advanced AI-driven payment prediction (Phase 2).
