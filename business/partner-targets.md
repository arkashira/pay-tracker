# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Pay-Tracker

### Target Integrations

1. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Widely used payment processing platform that handles complex invoicing and payment statuses.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: No free tier; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies payment tracking and collections for businesses using Stripe for transactions.

2. **QuickBooks Online**
   - **Rationale**: Popular accounting software that can streamline invoicing and payment status management.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 30-day free trial; paid plans start at $25/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Helps users manage their invoices and track payment statuses directly from their accounting software.

3. **PayPal**
   - **Rationale**: Major payment platform with extensive invoicing capabilities and user base.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: No monthly fees; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates payment tracking for users who frequently use PayPal for transactions.

4. **Xero**
   - **Rationale**: Cloud-based accounting software that offers invoicing and payment tracking features.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: No free tier; plans start at $13/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides seamless integration for users to manage invoices and track payment statuses.

5. **Square**
   - **Rationale**: Payment processing and point-of-sale system that includes invoicing features.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: No monthly fees; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies payment tracking for businesses using Square for sales and invoicing.

6. **FreshBooks**
   - **Rationale**: Accounting software focused on small businesses, with strong invoicing capabilities.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 30-day free trial; paid plans start at $15/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances invoicing and payment tracking for freelancers and small businesses.

7. **Zoho Invoice**
   - **Rationale**: Online invoicing software that allows users to create, send, and manage invoices.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free for up to 5 customers; paid plans start at $9/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides a straightforward solution for tracking payments and managing invoices.

8. **HubSpot**
   - **Rationale**: CRM platform that includes invoicing and payment tracking features.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier available with limited features; paid plans start at $50/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Helps businesses manage customer relationships while tracking payment statuses.

### Prioritization Criteria
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share Potential**: Focus on platforms with affiliate programs or revenue-sharing opportunities to enhance monetization.
- **User Job Solving**: Prioritize integrations that directly address the pain points of payment status tracking and invoicing complexities.
