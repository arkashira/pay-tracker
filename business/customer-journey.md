# Customer Journey Map for Pay-Tracker

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developers and businesses experience confusion and frustration with complex invoice payment statuses and split payments.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of visibility into payment statuses; existing solutions are cumbersome or inadequate.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, and overwhelm due to manual tracking and reconciliation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide clear educational content (blogs, webinars) on the importance of streamlined payment tracking; offer a free resource (e.g., a checklist) for managing invoices.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Users begin researching solutions to improve their payment tracking processes.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by the number of available solutions; difficulty in comparing features and pricing.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful but skeptical; uncertainty about which solution will truly meet their needs.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Create a comparison tool that highlights Pay-Tracker’s unique features and benefits; offer case studies or testimonials from similar businesses.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and engagement with comparison tools.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users sign up for a free trial or demo of Pay-Tracker.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; potential lack of guidance during the onboarding process.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety about the learning curve and effectiveness of the tool.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide a guided onboarding experience with tutorials and customer support; offer a checklist for setting up their account.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and time taken to complete onboarding.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users successfully integrate Pay-Tracker into their workflow and begin using it regularly.
- **Friction Points**: Potential for users to revert to old habits if they encounter issues or if the tool doesn’t meet expectations.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction if the tool meets their needs; frustration if they encounter bugs or usability issues.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular check-ins from customer success teams; proactive communication about updates and new features; provide a community forum for users to share tips.
- **Metric**: Monthly active users and user satisfaction scores.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users see the value in Pay-Tracker and consider additional features or upgrades.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about the ROI of upgrading; potential complexity in understanding advanced features.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in the tool’s value but cautious about additional investment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized recommendations based on user behavior; provide clear ROI examples for advanced features; create loyalty programs or discounts for upgrades.
- **Metric**: Upgrade conversion rate and customer lifetime value (CLV).
